A Legacy of Healing Since 2010

Samarth Sahyadri Multi-specialty Hospital was established with a singular mission: to bring world-class healthcare to the heart of Ghoti, Igatpuri, and surrounding communities. We believe everyone deserves access to state-of-the-art medical facilities delivered with profound empathy.

Under the guidance of our experienced medical directors, we strive to provide personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs, ensuring the highest standards of clinical outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, high-quality, and affordable multispeciality healthcare services to the local community. We are dedicated to clinical excellence, patient safety, and delivering holistic care that improves the overall well-being of the patients we serve in the Igatpuri region.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and advanced regional healthcare destination, recognized for pioneering medical treatments and unwavering compassionate patient care.
